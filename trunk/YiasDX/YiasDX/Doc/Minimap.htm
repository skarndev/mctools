Minimap - Selection of the entry point

The screen that shows up after you have pressed on one of the buttons indicating the different continents available to edit allows you to select where you would like to start editing the world. This screen will look similar to the following picture (depending on which continent you choose):

 

Actually its pretty simple. The map you see here was generated from the WDL-file of the selected continent. These files contain information about the height of the terrain and WoW uses those files to render the background when you are far away from a specific point. I use it to get the shape of the terrain ;). The black lines you see separate each ADT from the other ones so you know where the border of the ADT are. In the top left you see a little lable that shows you the X/Z - offset of the ADT that is currently under the mouse-pointer. This refers to the name (like Kalimdor_40_29.adt). If you would like to calculate the clientposition of that ADT just calculate X: x * 533.333 Y: Not set Z: z * 533.333. From there you get the coordinates of the server by calculating X: (32 - x) * 533.333 Y: (32 - z) * 533 Z: Not set.

As the text on the bottom shows you can move and zoom this map. The mousewheel lets you zoom in and out and by holding the right mousebutton and moving the mouse you can pan around when the map is zoomed in.

If you found the desired place just press the left mousebutton and loading starts!
